## Design direction

See `DESIGN.md` §7.3. Short form:

- **Logo:** stylized tricorne silhouette, three-quarter profile,
  firebrick cockade on the upturned brim
- **Colors:**
  - Primary violet `#4B0082`
  - Cockade red `#B22222`
  - Cream `#F5F1E8`
  - Matte black `#0A0A0A`
- **Lockups:** square icon mark; horizontal wordmark
- **Tone:** tactical, not edgy. Strategist, not pirate. The hat is
  the whole aesthetic.
